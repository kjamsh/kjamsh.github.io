I'm a PhD student at Simon Fraser University, supervised by Keval Vora. My research focuses on the interactions between systems and algorithms for big data processing, especially on graphs.

I built Peregrine, a fast and programmable graph mining system which analyzes user queries to aggressively optimize execution.
